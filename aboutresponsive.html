About This Website


The Problem And How It Is Solved

This website was inspired by the lack of current portfolio trackers that focus on the trader's risk management. As a matter of fact, there are currently no tools to efficiently track your trading with a focus on your risk. The existing applications for portfolio tracking have similarities and differences. For the most part, they organize all of the user's portfolios into one place, show how their capital is allocated among asset classes and specific underlying stocks and funds, provide alerts and news based on the user's holdings, and show charts and stock fundamentals. None of these tools will help a trader become better at their craft. When traders prioritize their risk management, they increase the probability of the succeeding as a trader or an investor and decrease the probability of losing money over time. There are so many trading styles and ways that traders find their edge in the market, but the one thing that is consistent among the greatest traders in history is their prioritization of managing their risk.


The Tools

This website offers tools designed to help traders track their trading performance while being able to easily analyze their risk. Based on the data that is tracked, a trader can see what works well for them, and what does not work. What does not work will appear as outliers in the tables and charts, so a trader can think back to the trade, and think why it went wrong, and continue doing what the tool is showing them works. If a trader sees large increases in their plots indicating profitability, they can hone in on what worked during these increases and continue doing this. Likewise, if a trader sees large drops in their plots indicating losses, the trader can analyze what went wrong and prevent that situation from happening to them again.


Why I Built This Platform

I built this platform becuase I did not see any platforms that track a trading portfolio with a focus on risk management. My trading journey led me to prioritze risk over anything else. When I did this, I began seeing consitent profits. I realized that the market is smarter than me and that I cannot predict or control where the price of a stock goes, I can only limit my downside by performing proper risk management. I built these tools for myself to track my own trading as it is the only tool I use to track my trading. Because of this and the fact that there was no existing tracker that suited my needs as a trader, I built this tool with the hope that other traders find value in it and not only imporve their own trading through it, but consistently outperform the indexes and compound their hard earned capital.


Who Is This For And Why Should They Use It

This is ideal for swing, position, and momentum traders who trade stocks. This is not the ideal tool for options traders, day traders, long term investors, or traders who trade FOREX, cryptocurrencies, or other asset classes. The tools on this platform were designed for the swing trader.

My Journey

I started trading stocks as a junior in high school. I started small because I knew so little, but wanted to get involved as early as possible. During this year, I managed to blow almost half of my portfolio off trading penny stocks with no understanding of trading. Many of these trades would even be day trades. I had no strategy and no rules. Before I knew it, and half of my initial investment was given back to the market, I decided to buy well established and well known companies that I thought were valuable and had a small chance of going to zero like tech and large cap to mega cap stocks. I would buy and hold these for over a year length and was able to bring my portfolio back to green territory. During my early college years I learned about technical analysis and fundamental analysis, as well as what makes a stock have potential to perform well. My trading during this time was better and profitable, but not spectacular, and definately not where I wanted it to be. It took me two years to become profitable, but those two years were some of my most valuable years trading in terms of learning from my mistakes. Two years ago I came across the CANSLIM methodology. I learned that company fundamentals are not enough, but a combination of fundamentals and technical analysis was what was needed to achieve superperformance. I studied William O'Neil, Mark Minervini, Jesse Livermore, and other market wizards. I learned to become more robotic in my trading and have rules phyiscally written out so that I can stick to them. The rules I learned about trading and risk management from market wizards inspired me to turn my tool into a platform for anyone to use. I am also an options seller and have an interest in selling options, but because it is best to stick to one strategy, I focus most of my efforts and hard earned capital in growth stock swing trading.
